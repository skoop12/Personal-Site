I am currently a graduate student in the Masters Program in Computer Science at The University of Chicago. Some of my favorite courses from the program have been Databases, Web Development, and iOS Application Development.

For my undergraduate degree, I went to The University of Notre Dame (go Irish!), where I received a B.B.A. in Information Technology Management.

I was born and raised in Minnesota. I moved to Indiana for college and briefly lived in London during a study abroad program. After graduating from Notre Dame, I moved to Chicago and have been living there for the past two years.

My goal is to obtain a software engineering role after graduation. I am interested in the areas of web development, mobile application development, fintech, database administration, and product management.

In my free time I love to travel (Croatia is the next on my bucket list) and try new restaurants (Tango Sur is my favorite in Chicago). I also enjoy doing yoga and paint by numbers.
